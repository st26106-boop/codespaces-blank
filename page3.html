Favourite Food

My favourite foods from the Philippines are Adobo and Sinigang. Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made by cooking chicken or pork in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, giving it a rich and savoury flavour. The meat becomes very tender and is usually eaten with rice, which makes the meal very satisfying. Sinigang is another traditional dish that is known for its sour and comforting soup, often made with tamarind, vegetables, and pork or fish. The sour taste makes it refreshing and perfect to eat on a cold or rainy day. I like these dishes because they are full of flavour and represent the delicious and unique food culture of the Philippines. 🍲🇵🇭
